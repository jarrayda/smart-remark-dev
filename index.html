Welcome to Smart Re-Mark v2.2

GPS Waypoint Deduplicator, Cleaner & Format Converter

You like fishing, you have a boat. You go out a lot. You make marks. You save spots.
Friends send you spots. Enemies send you spots. Over time you've got hundreds of them — and honestly, you don't know which ones are good, which are duplicates, or which are so close together there's no point keeping both.

We're here to help. Load your file and Smart Re-Mark sorts it all out — so you head out with only the marks that matter and catch more fish on your next trip.

  • 📂
    Load & auto-analyseDrop or browse a file — duplicates and conflicts are found immediately, no extra steps.
  • 🔁
    Exact & near duplicatesMarks at identical coordinates or within your distance threshold are grouped. Pick which ones to keep.
  • 🏷
    Same name, different placeMarks sharing a name but far apart are flagged. Rename or remove the ones you don't need.
  • ✏️
    Edit export namesClick any name in the table to set a custom export label — the original is always preserved.
  • ⚙️
    Export optionsNormalise to Proper Case, append today's date, choose coordinate format (DD, DDM, DMS), export as GPX, CSV or KML.
  • ✅
    Clean Preview & BinnedSee exactly which marks will be exported and which will be removed before you commit to anything.
  • 🗑
    Safe removalWhen you export, any removed marks are automatically saved to a separate BinnedMarks backup file.
  • 🔒
    Offline Safety Disconnect before loading your marks. Everything runs in your browser — nothing leaves your device. Reconnect after exporting and verify for yourself. How to clear your browser cache after use ↗

Smart Re-Mark — version history

v2.2 Mar 2026 Latest

  • Waypoint & Track toggle in header — switch between modes, each with its own sidebar and map
  • Track list shows distance, date/time span (AEST), keep/remove buttons per track
  • Track map uses Web Mercator projection for accurate alignment with satellite/terrain tiles
  • Satellite / Terrain map toggle on both waypoint and track maps
  • Track export modal — name your file, get a BinnedTracks backup automatically
  • Same-name conflict resolution deferred to export — no mid-session purge required
  • Date column (AEST) added to all waypoint tabs
  • Unified export — one GPX containing kept waypoints and kept tracks plus binned backup

  • Mobile-friendly layout — all modals become bottom sheets on small screens, tabs scroll horizontally, tables resize gracefully
